Delicious Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Glue
  • Laundry detergent
  • food coloring
  • water
  • glitter

Instructions:

  1. Fill a measuring cup with 1 cup of water and pour it into your mixing bowl.
  2. Mix in 1/2 cup of glue. (Add more or less depending on the consistency you want.)
  3. Squeeze a few drops of food coloring into the mixture.
  4. Add glitter or other mix-ins if you wish.
  5. Stir in about 1/4 cup of liquid laundry detergent.
  6. Stir until the slime starts to form a gooey texture.
  7. Stir in more laundry detergent until it's slimy enough for you.
  8. Knead the slime with both hands until its firm.
  9. Store your slime in an airtight plastic bag, reusable plastic container or glass jar.
