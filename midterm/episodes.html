season 1

1.Dexter

Dexter Morgan, a blood spatter analyst with a dark secret, hunts Miami’s criminals at night. He discovers a mysterious killer who leaves no blood and senses a strange connection.

2.Cocodrilo

Dexter pursues a man who murdered two college students. Meanwhile, the Ice Truck Killer, the serial killer Dexter's been following, leaves him a personal message.

3.Popping cherry

Dexter mentors a young, inexperienced killer while examining his own childhood memories. The Ice Truck Killer sends another eerie clue, pushing Dexter further.

4.Lets give the boy a hand

The Ice Truck Killer leaves dismembered body parts around Miami, all connected to Dexter's past. Dexter realizes the killer knows more about him than he thought.

5.Love American style

Dexter targets a human trafficker preying on Cuban immigrants. He wrestles with his girlfriend Rita’s desire for intimacy, struggling to balance his dual life.
